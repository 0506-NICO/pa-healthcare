Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

Appointments

How do I book an appointment?
Booking is easy! Simply click the "Book Appointment" button on our homepage, select your preferred service, choose a date and time that works for you, fill in your details, and complete the payment. You'll receive a confirmation email immediately.
Can I reschedule or cancel my appointment?
Yes! You can reschedule or cancel your appointment up to 24 hours before the scheduled time. Log into your account, go to "My Appointments", and select the appointment you wish to modify. For cancellations within 24 hours, please contact us directly.
What should I bring to my appointment?
Please bring a valid ID, your appointment confirmation (email or SMS), any relevant medical records or test results, and your payment receipt. If this is your first visit, arrive 10-15 minutes early to complete registration.

Payments

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept multiple payment methods including: Debit/Credit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Verve), Bank Transfers, USSD payments, and mobile money. All payments are processed securely through Paystack.
Is my payment information secure?
Absolutely! We use Paystack, a PCI-DSS compliant payment processor. Your card details are encrypted and never stored on our servers. All transactions are protected with bank-level security.
Can I get a refund?
Refunds are available for cancellations made at least 24 hours before your appointment. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to your original payment method. For emergencies, please contact our support team.

Account & Profile

How do I create an account?
Click the "Register" button on our website, fill in your name, email, phone number, and create a password. You'll receive a verification email - click the link to activate your account. That's it!
I forgot my password. What do I do?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your registered email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. The link expires in 1 hour for security reasons.

Services

What services do you offer?
We offer a comprehensive range of healthcare services including: General Consultation, Laboratory Tests, Specialized Care, Mental Health Services, Emergency Care, Eye Care, and more. Visit our Services page for detailed information and pricing.
Do you offer emergency services?
Yes, we provide emergency care services. For life-threatening emergencies, please call emergency services (112) or visit the nearest hospital immediately. For urgent but non-life-threatening issues, contact our emergency line.
